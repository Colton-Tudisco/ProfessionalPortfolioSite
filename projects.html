Manufacturing Production Planner (In-Progress)

An internal production planning web app built for the company I currently work for, Schlegel Electronic Materials, that turns raw ERP exports into actionable operational dashboards. The app processes sales orders, inventory, bills of materials, and purchase orders to give buyers, planners, and production managers a real-time picture of what can ship, what needs to be made, and what materials are short.

Features include order status tracking with days-to-ship and can-ship status, a job planning tool that generates full raw material requirements for a production run, purchasing alerts for material shortages, and a BOM attention panel that flags parts needing classification. Definitely my most ambitious project to date!

Tech used: React, Vite, FastAPI (Python), pandas
